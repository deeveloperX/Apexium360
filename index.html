Portfolio

We built a custom shipping Shopify app that generates rates specific to Outback Toy's needs with pre-order items. Our app talks to Shiphawk APl and returns the rates dynamically in their checkout. This app allows the company flexibility in their shipping options, unlike any other app in the industry.

We developed a custom shipping insurance widget that allows the store to collect additional revenue and protect themselves from lost shipment costs from their carrier. They were able to receive a 1200% ROl on this project so far and that will continue to grow over time.

The client had two primary issues that we were looking to solve: low conversion rates and high return rates. We designed and developed a completely custom product picking experience that walks the user through picking a color and appropriate sizes, leveraging homegrown dekstech UX/Ul code. This solution leads to lower return rates and better user experience, solving major problems for the client.

We completely overhauled the design with a beautiful UX that significantly increased conversion rate and visibility. We are helping with their Google Ads, creating, optimizing and ensuring they're getting the best results possible.

Partnering alongside Superside, we designed a website for an email agency. We paired this design with our Shopify development skills to create a beautiful website.

We built a completely custom Furniture Shopify solution, leveraging a data feed from one of the world's largest vendors, Wondersign. Our Shopify solution includes availability, warranty, and related items functionality. This experience creates a world-class user experiencing, leading to higher conversions.

We built a completely custom app that allows the user to upload their own logo, resize it and move it onto the envelop where they want it, right on the website! We also allow them to enter their address and other details. It's a custom Shopify app that we built from the ground up.

Unlike other web agencies, we always find a way to make things work based on our client's needs. This site is a prime example of us giving a non-profit the ability to collect donations in a very customized way through Shopify. We make the impossible possible with our wizard dev skills.

This client was facing an issue with conversion rates and not being able to sell online. We took their product page and converted it into a high-converting landing page that speaks to their target audience.

Since this client has several products over 100 options, we completely scrapped and rewrote a broken script to fix a critical bug for them. We used our knowledge of Shopify and intuitive approach to scripting to resolve it.

We built a custom homepage on Shopify for this client in the healthcare industry, focusing in on the key benefits and aspects of semaglutide.

We took this site from slow loading page speeds to under 3 seconds page load with 85 mobile and 99 desktop scores on Google (great for SEO). Our hands-on and no-tool approach allowed them to get the most out of this project.

We built a completely custom engagement ring builder, that allows the customer to see the ring real-time as they choose their options. They are able to go back and forth and see which look they like the best.

We built a highly custom data model that supports unique filtering of data on collection pages and completely unique product page experience that allows user to pick a predefined size or select a preset one with a custom price. We developed this solution using Shopify metaobjects, metafields and custom javascripting.

In order to better serve their customers, we built a custom cart drawer that slides out when someone clicks add to cart. We also developed our custom shipping insurance widget which has of course seen 5x - 6x ROl on their initial investment.

This client was frustrated with what Squarespace was offering and wanted to switch to Shopify. We brought that vision into reality with ease, creating an exact replica and copy on Shopify with all the same functionality.
